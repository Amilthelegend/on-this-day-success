Privacy Policy

On This Day - Historic Events Pro

Last updated: November 24, 2024

Summary

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information whatsoever.

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Information We DON'T Collect

This extension does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only using Chrome's storage API:

  • Pro License Status: Whether you have activated Pro features (Yes/No)
  • License Key: Your Pro license key (if purchased)
  • Saved Favorites: Historical events you've marked as favorites
  • Theme Preference: Your selected theme (Default, Dark, or Vintage)
  • Pro Activation Date: When you activated Pro features

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Historical Event Data

The extension fetches historical events from the public "On This Day" API. This API provides historical information and does not collect personal data.

Payment Processing

When you purchase Pro features, payment is processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment transactions.

Success Page Hosting

After payment, you're redirected to a success page hosted on GitHub Pages.

Permissions Explanation

activeTab Permission

Purpose: Opens the Stripe payment page when you click "Upgrade to Pro"

Usage: Only creates a new tab with the payment link. Does not access, read, or modify any webpage content.

storage Permission

Purpose: Saves your favorites and preferences locally on your device

Usage: Stores data using Chrome's local storage API. All data remains on your device.

No Tracking or Analytics

This extension does not:

  • Track your browsing activity
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Collect usage statistics
  • Share data with third parties
  • Display advertisements

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Data: All stored data can be viewed in the extension's Settings
  • Delete Data: Remove the extension to delete all stored data
  • Deactivate Pro: You can deactivate Pro features at any time from Settings
  • No Account Required: No sign-up or account creation needed

Data Security

All data is stored securely using Chrome's built-in storage API. Since no data is transmitted to external servers, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access from our end.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is appropriate for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
